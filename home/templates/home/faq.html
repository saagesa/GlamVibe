Are your products cruelty-free and vegan?

Absolutely — every GlamVibe product is 100% cruelty-free and never tested on animals. We are proud to say that the vast majority of our range is also fully vegan. Any product that contains a non-vegan ingredient (such as beeswax in certain lip balms) is clearly labelled on both the product page and the packaging so you can always shop with confidence.

We work closely with certified suppliers and undergo regular third-party audits to ensure our standards remain high across our entire supply chain. Glam without guilt — always.
